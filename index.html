The Batman

Bruce Wayne is a fictional character in the DC Comics universe, also known by his alter ego, Batman. Bruce Wayne is the billionaire owner of Wayne Enterprises. After witnessing the murder of his parents, Thomas and Martha Wayne, as a child, he vowed to rid Gotham City of crime. To do so, he uses his wealth, intelligence, and physical prowess to become Batman, a vigilante who fights crime with his advanced gadgets, combat skills, and cunning mind.
